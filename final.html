For our Final Project we decided on creating a game similar to "Flappy Bird" using our own Bitmojis as our characters. In this game you must navigate the bitmoji up and down through tubes while using the "Space" key. The objective of the game is to go for as long as possible without coming into contact with one of the tubes. During the game the tubes will gradually increase in speed flowing from right to left across the screen. Also as the time progresses the bitmoji character will speed up the rise and fall.
